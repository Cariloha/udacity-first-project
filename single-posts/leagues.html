Leagues I follow

By Berty Pérez

Thanks to the internet, now the women's football ir more reachable. You can watch it on streaming platforms, YouTube, or you local tv company. Adding that now the media is realizing something they took a lot of tinme to realize, women's football sells and it's really profitable, there are tons of women, children and also men that enjoy watching women's football. After this breaf introduction, here are the leagues I follow and also recommend you if you want to support women's football.

Liga BBVA femenil

This league is from México, and it's a "young" league, their first edition was in 2017 and the first cup was won by Pachuca, and at the beggining there were two separate groups 'A' and 'B'. After aproximately two years they decided to merge the group into a one group where all teams played against each other. It was in 2019 that the "liga" allowed foreig players into the league, this would help to raise the competition level among the players.

The team that has won more cups is Tigres UANL femenil from Nuevo Leon, with 5 cups.

Next is Rayadas from Monterrey, Chivas from Guadalajara and América from CDMX.

UEFA Women's Champions League

I was very impressed when I found out that there was a women's champions league, I heard about it on the radio and I was truly exited, by that time it was almost imposible to watch the games in my country, but I managed somehow to follow the matches. This year YouTube channel DAZN have the rights to stream the matches in the league, so following it will be easier (thank you DAZN).

The best players globally are mostly in this league, the competition level is high and their football is beautiful, the team that has won more cups is Olympic de Lyon, winning 6 cups. Barcelona is comming really strong this past year and PSG is also one of the best teams in this league

USA National Women's Soccer League

I would dare to say that this is the most important league from America (the continent), the best players from the continent are in this league. Most of the players in México would love to play in the NWSL because this would increase their football skills. I followed this league on twitch, but recently DAZN announced that they would also be streaming this games (for some countries).

This league started in 2013, and the team that has won more cups is Portland Thorns FC with 3 cups, next is FC Kansas City with 2.
