About me

Hello, I'm Berty, I'm from México and I'm a front-end developer, before I became a developer I was a Civil Engineer and worked as it 5 years. Working in construction really gave me valuable skills, I learned how to manage people and solve problems, deal with external contractors and team work. I'm truly grateful for this part in my career but I also realized it wasn't my passion (althought I really loved working in the construction field).

After 5 years of working as a Civil Engineer, something in my gut told me that it was not to late to persiut the career I always wanted to persuit since I first touched and learn to use a computer. Fortunately I has the support of my family and that's how I started this beautiful tech journey, I have zero regrets, developing is what I love, I love to learn new things, I love to make the web design happen in web.

After this introduction I want to share with you that not all of me is about my career, I'm very passionate about sports. I started to practice football soccer when I was 13 years old, the team I played with back in those days was called "Titanes" and we won three local championships. I played football soccer until I turned 19 years old; when I turned 19 I started to practice windsurf and I practice this sport around 5 years, I had to quit practicing in order to keep focus in college, the last year I practiced windsurf I was able to be part of the National Olympics representing Yucatán (a state in México).
